Welcome to the Chase!

At Thrill of the Chase, we're more than just a collectible store—we're a pillar for hobbyists, gamers, collectors, and fans of all ages. Founded and operated by passionate collectors who wanted to build a homegrown space for like-minded individuals in Pocono Pines, PA.

What We Offer

Trading Card Games (TCGs)

Pokemon, One Piece, Magic the Gathering, Dragon Ball Super and more!

Collectibles

Funko pops, trading cards, limited edition items, vintage & modern collectibles, comics, and more!

Events & Tournaments

Weekly and monthly tournaments, release events, and monthly community weekends

Upcoming Events

A Place to Connect

Trade, rip, battle, or relax. We're designed to be a space where the community is welcome.

Why We Started

Thrill of the Chase was created because we wanted to make an inclusive space for collectors and players alike while offering a good selection of products at a great price!

As long-time fans and collectors ourselves, we know exactly what it's like to finally hunt down that chase card you've been searching for or the exhilarating feeling of taking home the top spot. We want you to experience this thrill as well every time you visit.

Our Promise

Whether you're a seasoned hunter, a competitor, casual collector or a curious beginner, we're here to make your experience fun, friendly and enjoyable.

Community always comes first at Thrill of the Chase.
